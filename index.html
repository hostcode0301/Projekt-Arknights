Arknights

Operators

Exusiai

Exusiai is a citizen of Laterano and an employee at Penguin Logistics. Her duties include covert communication and armored vehicle transport, leading to speculation that she is, in fact, a Messenger. Penguin Logistics has assigned her to Rhodes Island, where she assists with various operations.

Saria

Saria was once the director of the Rhine Lab Defense Division. She has made contributions to the fields of life science, microbiology, and Originium Arts. At the same time, she has also proven herself formidable when it comes to demolition, protection, and anomaly containment missions. Currently, she is working with Rhodes Island in various scientific fields and is negotiating a more in-depth cooperation plan with them.
